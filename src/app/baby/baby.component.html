BABY SHOWERS

L.I.F.E. Baby Showers are the main aspect of our program. Having a baby is a really important time in your life no matter what age you are. Unfortunately for lots of teen parents, they either cannot afford to throw one or they don't have the family to come and attend. L.I.F.E Baby Showers Provide both the financial support and the company that any family would need to throw a baby shower.

We try our best to make sure that the events that we have are fun and a good time for both the attendees and the hosts of the party. We have games and music and so much more. Due to the fact that this is a highly important time, we are making sure that not only do people have the baby shower, but they also build connections and a support system that they can go to whether they are in the best or worst season of their life.
